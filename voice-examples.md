# Your Voice Corpus

A curated set of **your own best posts**, used as few-shot examples so the writer matches your voice. This is the other half of "training" the writer (alongside `voice-guide.md`).

**How to fill this in:**
1. Pick 15-20 of your real posts that performed well and sound most like you.
2. Paste each one's full text in a blockquote under a short label.
3. Add a one-line "Why it works" naming the mechanic (the hook shape, the structure, the turn).

The more real examples, the better the drafts. Thin corpus = generic drafts.
