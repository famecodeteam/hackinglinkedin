# Pillars, cadence & angle fuel

## Content pillars

- **Workplace** — culture, management, leadership, remote, hiring. Highest-engagement zone.
- **Business** — bootstrapping, finance, cashflow, productivity, self-development.
- **Marketing** — B2B marketing, your craft, case studies. (Case studies live here.)
- **AI** — building in public, vibe coding, shipping internal tools, replacing SaaS. Framed through time/cost saved and "you could build this yourself now."
- **Wildcard** — transparent CEO diary, unguarded founder moments.

Plus a content **type** used on Mondays:

- **Repost** — re-share a past post that did 500+ reactions. The `draft` field holds the original post URL to re-share.

## Weekday cadence

| Day | Slot |
|-----|------|
| Mon | Repost a past hit (500+ reactions) |
| Tue | Case Study |
| Wed | Rotating category |
| Thu | Rotating category |
| Fri | Rotating category |

Wed–Fri rotate through Workplace / Business / AI / Wildcard, shifting each week so nothing gets stale.

## Enemies (angle generators — argue against these)

CVs / cover letters, VCs & "scalable"-obsession, offices, boards, trade shows, cold email, interruption marketing, micromanagement.

## Hot topics

Remote work, leadership, LinkedIn, writing, burnout, job hunting.

Enemies and hot topics are raw material for hooks — not pillars themselves.
