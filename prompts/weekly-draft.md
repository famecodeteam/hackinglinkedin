# Weekly draft — prompt

Use this once a week to draft the week's posts in your voice.

---

Read `voice-guide.md` and `pillars.md` first.

Then, from `data/ideas.json`, take the ideas I point you at (or pick the strongest unslotted idea for each weekday per the cadence in `pillars.md`). For each:

1. Write a full LinkedIn post in your voice (per voice-guide.md + voice-examples.md) — strong 'see more' hook, one idea per line, real numbers, no hashtags, 100-300 words.
2. Set the item's `draft` to the full text, `status` to `"drafted"`, and `day` to the weekday you're slotting it to.
3. For **Repost** items, don't rewrite — the `draft` already holds the original URL to re-share.
4. For **Case Study** items, keep them in the case-study format; only tidy, don't force into personal voice.

Save changes to `data/ideas.json`. Then show me each draft so I can finish and post it manually. Do NOT post anything.
